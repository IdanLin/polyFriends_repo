# PolyFriends

## 1. Project Overview

**PolyFriends** is a user-friendly and interactive Full-Stack platform designed for the complete management of social bets and wagers within a closed circle of friends. 

Social and interactive bets (such as predicting sports match results, daily events, and casual wagers) are common within friend groups. PolyFriends provides a dedicated space to manage these interactions smoothly, replacing manual tracking with an organized, automated digital ledger.

## 2. Key Features

The system offers a comprehensive suite of tools to enhance the social betting experience:
* **Automated Ledger:** Automatically calculates financial balances, winnings, and debts among different group members, saving time and preventing arguments.
* **Centralized Hub:** Keeps track of all open and closed bets, neatly organized by categories.
* **Real-time Tracking:** Participants can view their current balance, betting history, and net winnings on a dynamic leaderboard.
* **User-Friendly Interface:** Provides convenient search options and easy filtering for users to find specific bets quickly.

## 3. Project Goals

The main goal of the PolyFriends system is to create a fun, transparent, and seamless environment for friends to engage in friendly wagers. By eliminating the manual overhead of managing debts and tracking who picked which option, the platform allows users to focus purely on the excitement of the bets themselves.
